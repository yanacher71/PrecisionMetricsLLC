Solutions

Smart devices are becoming softer, thinner, and more adaptive — and they demand equally adaptive precision.

From AR glasses to next-gen soft-bodied smartphones, today’s devices experience micro-deformations during everyday use. These deformations — caused by heat, movement, pressure, or wear — need to be measured and adjusted for in real time to maintain performance, comfort, and reliability.

We develop ultra-precise, miniaturized sensors designed to detect and quantify these micro-deformations. Our system integrates with machine learning algorithms that analyze behavior patterns and calculate the precise adjustments needed to optimize device function over time.

Every product has unique physical tolerances — so our solution is customizable per device type, material profile, and user context.

  • ML models trained on device-specific deformation signatures
  • Real-time output for actuator or interface tuning
